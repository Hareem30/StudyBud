Welcome to Study Buddy

Struggling alone is out collaborating is in! StudyBud connects students in similar courses based on how they learn, when they study, and what helps them thrive. Whether you're a visual learner, a night owl, or a flashcard fanatic, StudyBud finds your perfect match. Why you'll love it:

📝 Shared Notes & Learning Styles – Sync up with peers who study like you.

📅 Smart Study Scheduling – Plan sessions that actually fit your life.

🔗 Resource Sharing – Swap guides, flashcards, and thoughts of wisdom.
